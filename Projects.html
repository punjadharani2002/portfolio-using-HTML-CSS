Project Name :-

Soil Moisture Detection Using Arduino UNO

Project Guide :-

Mrs.M.Anitha

Description

The main motto of this project is to measure the moisture of the soil or water content in soil .The soil Moisture sensor is used to measure the volumetric water content of soil.It performing experiments in courses such as soil science,agricultural science,environmental science.Arduino uno microcontroller is the main controlling unit of whole system.

Working Principle

● In this project we are using resistive soil moisture
● A resistive soil moisture sensor works by using the relationship between electrical resistance and water content to gauge the moisture level of the soil,we will observe the sensor has to exposed probes that are inserted directly into the soil.
● A electrical current is sent from one probe to other which allows the sensor to measure the resistance of the soil between them.
● When the water content in soil is high, it has a higher electrical conductivity, which indicates high soil moisture.
● When the water content in the soil is low , it has poor electrical conductivity, which indicates low soil moisture .
